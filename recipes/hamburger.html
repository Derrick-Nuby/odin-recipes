Hamburger Recipe

Get ready to sink your teeth into a juicy and flavorful burger that will satisfy your cravings and leave you craving more. With a combination of succulent meat, melty cheese, and mouthwatering toppings, this homemade burger is the epitome of comfort food. Follow the simple steps below to create a burger that will surpass your expectations and become a favorite in your household.

ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your favorite cheese)
  • Burger toppings of your choice (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, etc.)
  • Condiments of your choice (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, barbecue sauce, etc.)

Steps and procedures

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, gently combine the ground beef, salt, and black pepper. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a tougher burger. Form the mixture into four equal-sized patties, slightly larger than the size of your burger buns.
  3. Place the burger patties on the preheated grill or pan. Cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness is reached. For medium-rare, aim for an internal temperature of 135°F (57°C), medium at 145°F (63°C), and well-done at 160°F (71°C).
  4. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on top of each patty to allow it to melt.
  5. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble your burger by placing a cooked patty with melted cheese on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Add your desired toppings, such as lettuce, tomato slices, onion, and pickles, on top of the patty.
  8. Drizzle or spread your favorite condiments, such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or barbecue sauce, on the top half of the bun.
  9. Place the top half of the bun on your burger, gently pressing it down to hold everything together.
  10. Serve your delicious homemade burgers with a side of fries, coleslaw, or any other accompaniments of your choice.
